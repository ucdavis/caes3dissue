A CLOSER LOOK
As a senior majoring in human development,
Andi Vilaboy works with preschoolers at the
Early Childhood Lab School on the UC Davis
campus. The Center for Child and Family Studies
gives undergraduates an opportunity to experience
what it’s like to work with young children and to
develop skills in translating theory and research
into effective interactions.
