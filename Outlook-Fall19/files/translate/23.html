Helping hands
Mauricio Guerrero-Villanueva, a clinical nutrition major, gets ready to volunteer at the Knights Landing
One Health Center Medical Clinic. Twice each month, the clinic unites UC Davis undergraduates, graduate
students and medical students with physicians and nurses to provide free healthcare to the medically
underserved in Knights Landing, La Tijera, Robbins and nearby migrant camps. Many student volunteers
are preparing for careers in healthcare. Undergraduate students act as interpreters, patient advocates,
receptionists and lab workers. UC Davis operates similar clinics throughout the Sacramento region to
improve access to healthcare for uninsured and low-income populations.
