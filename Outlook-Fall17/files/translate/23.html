Ebb and Flow
UC Davis environmental science students take water samples from Suisun Marsh, a tidal estuary where
freshwater from the Sierras meets saltwater from San Francisco and creates a brackish mix. Suisun Marsh
is an essential part of the Delta ecosystem that connects the Sacramento and San Joaquin rivers to the
San Francisco Bay. The limnology field class, taught by CA&ES Professor Steven Sadro, helps students
develop an understanding of inland aquatic ecosystems.
